I participated in the 19th WFD congress (more exactly as a volunteer interpreter) in this competition, in 135 countries and almost 2,000 people participated. Various kinds of languages exsisted in this competition. Sign-language, and various kinds of languages from the 135 countries were mixed in this competition. In the competitions begining.
